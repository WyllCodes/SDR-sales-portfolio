# SDR Sales Pipeline Framework

Typical outbound sales pipeline:

Prospect → Contacted → Replied → Qualified → Meeting Booked → Opportunity

---

## Stage Breakdown

### Prospect
Lead identified using LinkedIn or lead databases.

### Contacted
Cold email or LinkedIn message sent.

### Replied
Prospect responds to outreach.

### Qualified
Prospect confirms interest and matches ICP.

### Meeting Booked
Call scheduled with Account Executive.

### Opportunity
Deal enters sales pipeline.
